Mission

The mission of our project is to provide excellent service using knowledge gained from TSU, for the proper management collection and processing of plastic waste.

Being a socially responsible and influential project through technological innovations linked to its users in the culture of recycling assuring that sustainable development, promoting the protection and improvement of the environment.

Be an alternative to the integrated waste management, acting for the greater good of our university, region and state, providing a cleaner natural environment, a sense of identity and an opportunity for cooperation with the environment for users;

they are recognized for our skills and seeking the welfare of future generations.
